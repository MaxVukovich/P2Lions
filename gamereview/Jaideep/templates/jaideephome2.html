Mario Cart

Mario Cart is a series of racing games developed and published by Nintendo in which users compete in races with different characters in an attempt to complete a certain amount of circuits before the other characters. It had first been released in August 27, 1992 and it was designed by Shigeru Miyamoto, Hideki Konno, Satoru Iwata, Takashi Tezuka, Tadashi Sugiyama, Yugo Hayashi, and Taku Matoba.

Need for Speed

Need for Speed is a series of racing games developed by Criterion Games and published by Electronic Arts which centers around street racing and the users have to complete many different races and escape the law enforcement in game. It had first been released in August 31, 1994 and it was designed by Electronic Arts, Andy Tudor, and Riley Cooper.

Rocket League

Rocket League is a game which was both developed and published by Psyonix in which two teams of rocket powered vehicles play soccer and attmept to score goals in the opponents goal. It had first been released in July 7, 2015.

Forza

Forza is a series of stimulation racing games which mainly focuses on profesional racing events and imitates the performance for many real life produced cars. It had first been released in May 3, 2005 and was designed by Ben Thaker-Fell
